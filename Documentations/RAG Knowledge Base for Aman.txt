RAG Knowledge Base for Aman

Tier 1 — Core (Non-Negotiable)
These directly power Aman's ability to respond safely and helpfully.

1. Psychology & Counseling Textbooks
The backbone of Aman's therapeutic reasoning.

    Cognitive Behavioral Therapy: Basics and Beyond — Judith Beck
        2nd Edition: https://img3.reoveme.com/m/be38edbbfc79330a.pdf

    Motivational Interviewing — Miller & Rollnick (critical for guiding resistant users)
        2nd Edition: https://bluepeteraustralia.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/motivational-interviewing.pdf

    Dialectical Behavior Therapy Skills Training — Marsha Linehan (emotional regulation)
        2nd Edition: https://mindsplain.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/DBT_handouts.pdf

    A Guide to Rational Living — Albert Ellis (REBT framework)
        https://pdfcoffee.com/qdownload/a-guide-to-rational-living-first-edition-albert-ellis-amp-robert-harper-pdf-free.html

    OpenStax Psychology (full textbook, peer-reviewed)
        openstax.org/details/books/psychology-2e

    Introduction to Psychology
        https://resources.saylor.org/wwwresources/archived/site/textbooks/Introduction%20to%20Psychology.pdf

    Self Help Resources for Mental Health Problems  (NOT done yet)
        https://www.cci.health.wa.gov.au/resources/looking-after-yourself

    The Principles of Psychology, Volume 1 (of 2) by William James
        https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/57628

    Safe Messaging Guidelines by AFSP and IASP
        LEADERS SUICIDE PREVENTION SAFE MESSAGING GUIDE
            https://www.dspo.mil/Portals/113/Documents/DSPO_LSP_SafeMsgGuid%20(Final).pdf

            https://aclanthology.org/2025.emnlp-main.1512.pdf

    Local Arabic crisis hotline numbers and their protocols


    CAMS (Collaborative Assessment and Management of Suicidality) framework
        National Strategy for Suicide Prevention
            https://www.hhs.gov/sites/default/files/national-strategy-suicide-prevention.pdf

    AFSP Safe Messaging Guidelines
        https://www.datocms-assets.com/12810/1770224577-talking_safely_about_suicide_flyer_rgb_m1_15036.pdf

Aman must know exactly when and how to escalate, what language is safe to use around self-harm, and what to absolutely avoid saying.

Tier 2 — High Value (Strongly Recommended)
These make Aman genuinely useful for everyday emotional struggles.

4. Therapeutic Techniques & Coping Strategies
Practical tools Aman can actually teach users during a session.

    CBT thought records and cognitive restructuring worksheets
    Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR) exercises
    Grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 sensory method, box breathing, etc.)
    ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy) metaphors and exercises
    Psychoeducation content — explaining what anxiety/depression feels like in plain language

    Therapist Aid Worksheets (free tier, large library)

    Palouse Mindfulness Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction
        https://palousemindfulness.com/docs/manual-print-single.pdf

    Dialectical behavior therapy - wikipedia
        https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dialectical_behavior_therapy

---------------------------------------------------------
5. Peer-Reviewed Research Papers
Gives Aman factual grounding when users ask "why" questions.

    Papers on anxiety, depression, grief, loneliness, burnout, and sleep disorders
    Research on the effectiveness of specific coping techniques
    Studies on emotional regulation in Arabic-speaking populations
    Trauma and PTSD literature (especially cultural trauma in MENA context)


Focus on abstracts + conclusions for RAG — full papers are too dense and noisy for retrieval. Chunk them smartly.

---------------------------------------------------------
6. Psychoeducation & Plain-Language Mental Health Content
For users who are confused, in denial, or first-timers to mental health conversations.

WHO & APA public-facing factsheets on common conditions
"What is anxiety/depression/grief?" explainers written for general audiences
Arabic-language mental health awareness content (e.g., from Takamol, Nafsi, or Shezlong platforms)
Myths vs. facts documents about mental health (especially important for Arabic cultural context where stigma is high)

WHO Mental Health Fact Sheets
    https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/mental-health-strengthening-our-response

Tier 3 — Contextual Enrichment
These fill in the gaps and make Aman culturally and practically aware.

7. Cultural & Religious Sensitivity Data (Critical for Arabic Users)
This is something most Western mental health systems miss — Aman shouldn't.

    Islamic perspectives on mental health, grief, and anxiety (scholarly, not pop content)
    Cultural stigma patterns around mental health in Arab societies
    How to frame mental health support within a faith-based worldview
    Arabic-specific emotional vocabulary (many Arabic emotional states have no English equivalent)

    دليل تدخالت برنامج رأب الفجوة في الصحة النفسية

So Aman can point users to real help.

9. Conversational Counseling Dialogue Datasets
Teaches Aman how to talk, not just what to know.

    Counseling and Psychotherapy Transcripts — public academic datasets
    EmpatheticDialogues dataset (Facebook Research) — empathetic response training data
    MEMO / CounselChat datasets — real therapist Q&A data
    Synthetic Arabic counseling dialogues (you may need to generate these)


This is especially useful if you fine-tune later, but also helps RAG understand the format and tone of supportive responses.
